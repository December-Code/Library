Reference Service

1.Research consultation

The Library offers a one-on-one research consultation service to all NTUST faculty members and students to assist them in

-Identifying information needs

-Locating resources most relevant to their research projects

-Constructing effective search techniques

-Searching electronic and print resources effectively

-Evaluating the resources used

Other Research Assistance

You can contact the reference librarian directly for research assistance or by email, phonecall..etc.

General and specific workshops and seminars are arranged from time to time. Please check our programs and register.

2.E-Resource Service

The library has subscribed to a wide variety of online resources including e-books,

periodicals and newspapers, thesis and dissertations, conference proceedings,

encyclopedias, and multimedia databases.

Discovery System is a one-stop search system for large amounts of print and online content, including most of NTUST’S collections.

Users could easily access to our e-resources in campus or outside the campus.
